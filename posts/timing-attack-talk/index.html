Timing Attack Talk

I gave a talk for our CS society on how timing attacks work. No prior knowledge on cryptography is required! Check me out :D

Here are random resources I thought of in the past 5 minutes that might be interesting:

  • The writeup / tutorial for timing attack on RSA can be found on Mystiz’s blog
  • The writeup for the timeless timing attack can be found on ConnorNelson’s blog
  • Writeups on performing power trace analysis can be found on Riatre’s gist
  • This talk on RSA low-bandwith acoustic cryptanalysis is a classic. You can extract private keys by listening to the CPU
  • If possible, I highly recommend you to get your hands on a device and try to mount these attacks yourself!
