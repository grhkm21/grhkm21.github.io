[NT] Little Ashish's Huge Donation

October 09, 2021

Source of problem: HackerRank.

1. Summary of Problem Statement

To briefly summarise, there are $T\leq 10^5$ test cases.

For each test case, we are given an integer $X\leq 10^{16}$. We are required to calculate the largest integer $n$ such that the sum of (square of each integer $\leq n$) is at most $X$.

2. First approach

Testing. $\sum_{i=1}^n i=\frac{n(n+1)}{2}$.

3. Lagrange Interpolation

$$y=\sum_{i=1}^n y_i\prod_{j\neq i} \frac{x-x_j}{x_i-x_j}$$
